Youtube Channel

SPADE A KPOP DANCE TEAM (2022-PRESENT) | Performance Coordinator

Youtube Channel

Spade A is a KPOP Dance cover team founded in Spring 2018 as the first dance group that features K-POP at USC. In addition to covering countless top trending K-POP songs, Spade A perform both on campus and off campus. Spade A has collaborated with companies such as 99rising, Hello82, PUBG Mobile, and SM Entertainment.

As Spade A's performance coordinator, I arrange performances at various events including at USC's Visions and Voices K-POP event, Songfest, and Korean Culture Night. We also won a competition held by Krazy Super Concert and got to perform on-stage at the event. Spade A also holds its own end of semester showcase.
